Our Mission:


"God's Word for Warriors" is an organization that recognizes the challenges veterans face resulting from their military service. This program addresses the spiritual component of veterans' lives, while confronting the key challenges they face on college campuses, in churches and in the community. Our goal is to provide a sense of Hope & Peace through the teachings of Jesus Christ.
